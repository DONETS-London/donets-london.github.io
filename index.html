About DONETS

Join us for the first edition of DONETS: Discovering the Opportunities of Network Science!

DONETS is a new workshop that aims to bring together PhD students and early-career researchers from related disciplines who use, or are interested in using, network-based methods to explore complex systems. The goal of DONETS is to provide a welcoming and supportive environment to present work, engage with peers across disciplines, and to foster connections and collaborations. Whether you're already familiar with network science, or just curious about its potential, you're warmly invited to take part.

The program will feature keynote and submitted talks, interactive activities and dedicated sessions for participants to share their work, discover common research interests, and build a strong and vibrant interdisciplinary community.

We welcome contributions from a wide variety of topics; check the submission page (to be opened) for details.
